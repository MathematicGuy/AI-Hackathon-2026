# Product Requirements

This directory contains approved product requirements and milestone baselines.
Requirements define customer outcomes and scope within the accepted product
contract and Accepted ADRs.

- [`air-conditioner-advisor-m1-prd.md`](air-conditioner-advisor-m1-prd.md) is
  the Milestone 1 product requirements document.
- [`business-viability-pilot-pathway-m1.md`](business-viability-pilot-pathway-m1.md)
  is the Vietnamese business-KPI, viability, and pilot-pathway baseline for
  Milestone 1 (additive; judge- and partner-facing).

Read the applicable requirements after the product contract and before product
architecture, story planning, or implementation.
